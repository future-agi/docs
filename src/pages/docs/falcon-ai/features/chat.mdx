## About

Falcon AI runs as a chat interface inside the Future AGI dashboard. It can be opened as a sidebar from any page for quick questions, or as a full-page view for longer conversations. The sidebar stays open while navigating between pages, so context is never lost. Conversations are saved automatically and can be resumed later.

---

## When to use

- **Quick questions from any page**: Open the sidebar to ask about what is on screen without leaving the current view.
- **Long investigations**: Switch to full-page mode for multi-step analysis where the sidebar feels too narrow.
- **Resuming past work**: Open conversation history to pick up where a previous session left off.

## How to

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Falcon AI">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Sidebar">
        Press `Cmd+K` (Mac) / `Ctrl+K` (Windows/Linux) to open a 420px overlay on the right side of the dashboard. It stays open across page navigations.
        ![Open Falcon AI sidebar](/screenshot/product/falcon/1.png)
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Full Page">
        Click **Falcon AI** in the navigation sidebar to open the full-page view at `/dashboard/falcon-ai`. Includes a chat history panel on the left for managing conversations.
        ![Open Falcon AI full page](/screenshot/product/falcon/2.png)
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ask a question">
    Type a question in the input area and press Enter. Falcon AI automatically detects what page is open and uses it as context.

    ![Ask a question](/screenshot/product/falcon/3.png)

     To ask about a different page, navigate there first or specify the page in your question (e.g. "In the evaluation page, which model performed best?").
    To add context manually, click **+ Add context** to attach specific entities (evaluations, datasets, traces). Up to 5 at a time.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use quick actions or slash commands">
    On a new chat, quick action buttons appear: **Analyze with compass**, **Create custom views**, **Build a dataset**, **Create an evaluation**, **Run simulation for my agent**. They disappear after the first message.
    ![Quick action buttons](/screenshot/product/falcon/4.png)

    Type `/` at the start of a message to open the command picker with available commands and custom skills.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Follow the response">
    Responses stream token-by-token with Markdown formatting. When Falcon AI calls tools, collapsible cards show each step with a checkmark (done), spinner (in progress), or warning (error).
    ![Response with tool calls](/screenshot/product/falcon/5.png)

     When an action creates or modifies a platform entity, a **completion card** appears with a one-click link to the result.

    When an action creates or modifies a platform entity, a **completion card** appears with a one-click link to the result.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Resume a past conversation">
    In the sidebar, click the **history** (clock) button. In full-page mode, the left panel shows all conversations with search. Conversations can be renamed or deleted via right-click.
    ![Conversation history](/screenshot/product/falcon/6.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
