## Safety & moderation

Evals in this category focus on **safety and moderation**: detecting harmful or toxic language, PII, prompt-injection attempts, and ensuring content is appropriate (e.g. safe for work, no harmful therapeutic advice).

| Eval | Description | Inputs | Output | Use case |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Content Moderation (Deprecated) | Flags potentially harmful, inappropriate, or unsafe content against safety guidelines. Use **Toxicity** instead. | `output` | Pass/Fail | Pre-release or live content moderation. |
| Content Safety Violation (Deprecated) | Detects harmful, unsafe, or prohibited content that violates safety guidelines. Use **Toxicity** instead. | `output` | Pass/Fail | Enforcing safety policies on model output. |
| Toxicity | Assesses content for offensive, abusive, or harmful language. | `output` | Pass/Fail | Keeping outputs safe for users. |
| Sexist | Detects gender bias or discriminatory content. | `output` | Pass/Fail | Ensuring inclusive, non-discriminatory output. |
| Is Harmful Advice | Checks if content contains guidance that could lead to harm if followed. | `output` | Pass/Fail | Blocking dangerous or risky advice. |
| No Harmful Therapeutic Guidance | Ensures no inappropriate or harmful medical, psychological, or therapeutic advice. | `output` | Pass/Fail | Safe health/wellness content. |
| Prompt Injection | Detects attempts to manipulate or bypass model behaviour via crafted inputs. | `input` | Pass/Fail | Securing prompt handling and APIs. |
| PII | Identifies personally identifiable information. | `input` | Pass/Fail | Privacy and compliance (e.g. redaction). |
| Data Privacy | Assesses alignment with privacy regulations (e.g. GDPR, HIPAA). | `output` | Pass/Fail | Compliance and data protection. |
| Safe for Work Text Eval | Ensures text is appropriate for professional environments (no NSFW content). | `output` or `text` | Pass/Fail | Workplace or professional channels. |
