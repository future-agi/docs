## Quality, tone & helpfulness

Evals in this category assess **quality, tone, and helpfulness**: tone and sentiment, politeness, conciseness, completeness, instruction adherence, task completion, conversation flow, and summary quality.

| Eval | Description | Inputs | Output | Use case |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Tone | Analyses tone and sentiment of content for emotional context and intent. | `output` | Choices (e.g. neutral, joy, anger) | Brand voice, audience fit. |
| Is Polite | Checks for politeness, respect, and appropriate social etiquette. | `output` | Pass/Fail | Customer-facing or support bots. |
| Is Informal Tone | Detects informal or casual tone (e.g. slang, contractions, emoji). | `output` | Pass/Fail | Matching desired formality. |
| Clinically Inappropriate Tone | Assesses tone for clinical or healthcare settings. | `output` | Pass/Fail | Medical or clinical applications. |
| Is Helpful | Evaluates whether the response helps solve the user's problem or question. | `input`, `output` | Pass/Fail | Support, QA, chatbots. |
| Is Concise | Checks that the response is concise and to the point. | `output` | Pass/Fail | Avoiding unnecessary verbosity. |
| Completeness | Evaluates whether the response fully addresses the input query. | `input`, `output` | Score | Ensuring nothing is left unanswered. |
| Prompt Instruction Adherence | Measures how closely output follows given prompt instructions. | `output`, `prompt` | Score | Instruction-following and constraints. |
| Task Completion | Evaluates whether the response successfully completes the requested task. | `input`, `output` | Score or Pass/Fail | Task-oriented agents or flows. |
| Conversation Coherence | Evaluates logical flow and context maintenance in a conversation. | `conversation` | Score | Multi-turn dialogue quality. |
| Conversation Resolution | Checks that each user query receives an appropriate and complete response. | `conversation` | Score | Resolving each turn in a conversation. |
| Is Good Summary | Evaluates whether a summary captures key information from the source. | `input`, `output` | Pass/Fail or score | Summarization quality. |
| Summary Quality | Evaluates whether a summary captures main points, maintains accuracy, and preserves meaning. | `input`, `output` | Score | Document or conversation summaries. |
| Not Gibberish | Assesses whether text is meaningful and coherent rather than random or incoherent. | `output` or `text` | Pass/Fail | Basic readability and coherence. |
