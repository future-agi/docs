## What they are

**Built-in evals** are pre-configured evaluation templates you can attach to dataset runs, prompt runs, and simulations. Each template defines what to measure (e.g. factual accuracy, tone, toxicity) and how to score it. You pick the evals you need, add them to your run, and the platform runs them automatically and surfaces results in the dashboard.

They are grouped by **category** below so you can find evals by use case. An eval can appear in **more than one category** when it fits multiple use cases.
