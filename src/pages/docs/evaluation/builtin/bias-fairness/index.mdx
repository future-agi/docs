## Bias & fairness

Evals in this category help detect **bias** and promote **fairness**: gender, age, racial, cultural, or general bias in model output.

| Eval | Description | Inputs | Output | Use case |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Bias Detection | Identifies gender, racial, cultural, or ideological bias; assesses balanced perspectives. | `output` | Pass/Fail or score | Auditing outputs for bias. |
| Cultural Sensitivity | Analyses output for cultural appropriateness, inclusive language, and cultural nuances. | `output` | Pass/Fail or score | Global or diverse audiences. |
| No Age Bias | Checks that content does not contain age-related bias or stereotypes. | `output` | Pass/Fail | Age-inclusive content. |
| No Gender Bias | Checks that content does not contain gender-related bias or stereotypes. | `output` | Pass/Fail | Gender-inclusive content. |
| No Racial Bias | Checks that content does not contain racial bias or discriminatory content. | `output` | Pass/Fail | Inclusive, non-discriminatory content. |
