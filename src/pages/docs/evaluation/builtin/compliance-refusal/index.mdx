## Compliance & refusal

Evals in this category cover **compliance and refusal**: whether the model correctly refuses harmful or inappropriate requests, and whether content follows guidelines or acceptable-use policies.

| Eval | Description | Inputs | Output | Use case |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Answer Refusal | Checks that the model properly refuses to answer harmful, dangerous, or inappropriate requests. | `input`, `output` | Pass/Fail | Safety and refusal behaviour. |
| Is Compliant | Evaluates whether content follows guidelines, standards, and acceptable use policies. | `output` (optional `context`) | Pass/Fail | Policy and compliance checks. |
