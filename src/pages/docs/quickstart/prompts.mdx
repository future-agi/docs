The Future AGI Prompt Workbench allows you to design, test, and optimize prompts for your AI applications. Whether you're starting from scratch or using templates, the workbench provides tools for prompt engineering and experimentation.

## Key Features

- **Model Selection**: Choose from various AI models (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.)
- **Parameter Tuning**: Configure temperature, top-p, max tokens, and more
- **Tool Integration**: Add tools to enhance your prompt's capabilities
- **Version Control**: Track changes and iterate on your prompts
- **Tracing**: Link prompts to observability traces for debugging

<Tip>
Access the Prompt Workbench from the [Future AGI Dashboard](https://app.futureagi.com) under the "Build" section.
</Tip>
