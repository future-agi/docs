## What it is

**Creating a prompt with AI** (Generate with AI) means describing what you want your prompt to do in plain language; the platform then generates system and user prompt content for you. You provide a **statement** (e.g. “Write a professional email using the recipient’s name and topic”) and optionally a **follow-up** to refine the result. The generated prompt appears in the editor where you can edit, run, and save it. Use this when you want a first draft quickly without writing from scratch or picking a template.

---

## Use cases

- **Quick first draft** — Get prompt text generated from a short description so you can refine instead of starting from a blank page.
- **Idea exploration** — Try different descriptions (e.g. “summarize in bullet points”, “tone: formal”) and iterate on the generated prompt.
- **Refinement** — Use the optional follow-up (e.g. “make it more concise”) to steer the generation without rewriting everything.
- **Learning** — See how the AI structures prompts for your use case and reuse or adapt the pattern.

## How to

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Prompts Section">
    From the Future AGI dashboard, locate the navigation panel on the left. Under **Build**, click **Prompts** to open the prompts management interface.
    {/* Image not available: from-ai/1.png */}
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start Generate with AI">
    In the Prompts section, click **Create prompt** on the right. In the “Create a new prompt” modal, select **Generate with AI** (instead of “Start from scratch” or “Start with a template”).
    {/* Image not available: from-ai/2.png */}
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your statement">
    Describe what you want the prompt to do in a short statement. For example:
    - “Write a professional email using the recipient’s name and topic”
    - “Summarize the following text in bullet points”
    - “Answer customer support questions in a friendly, concise way”
    {/* Image not available: from-ai/3.png */}

  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for generation to complete">
    The platform generates the prompt based on your statement (and follow-up if provided). In the UI, generation may stream progress. When it finishes, the prompt editor opens with the generated **system** and **user** content filled in.
    {/* Image not available: from-ai/4.png */}
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review, edit, and run">
    In the prompt editor you can:
    - **Rename** the prompt and **choose a model** (and parameters) if needed.
    - **Edit** the generated system and user text, add variables in `{{brackets}}`, or adjust formatting.
    - **Run Prompt** to test the prompt and see the model’s response.
    {/* Image not available: from-ai/5.png */}

    Save when you’re satisfied, or iterate by changing the statement and generating again.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Optional next steps">
    After generating your prompt, you can:
    - **Save it as a template** — Reuse the prompt (or a tuned version) as a template for your team.
    - **Iterate** — Change the statement or follow-up and regenerate to try different drafts.
    - **Create from scratch or from template** — Use [Create from scratch](/docs/prompt/create-prompt-from-scratch) or [Use existing templates](/docs/prompt/create-prompt-from-existing-template) for other workflows.
  </Step>
</Steps>
