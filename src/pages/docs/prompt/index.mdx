## What it is

Prompts are the instructions you give to AI models to get the responses you need. Without a single place to manage them, prompts end up scattered across code or config, changes are hard to track, and it’s difficult to compare versions or run them consistently against datasets and evals. That’s why we built **Prompt Workbench**: a place in Future AGI where you create, test, and refine these instructions for optimal results. With built-in templates, version control, and performance tracking, you can systematically improve your AI interactions over time.

## Purpose

- **Create and manage prompts** — Build prompts from scratch or from existing templates, with full control over structure and variables.
- **Organize and navigate** — Use folders to group prompts and keep your workspace manageable.
- **Track and optimize** — Link prompts to traces for monitoring and use version history to compare and roll back changes.
- **Integrate with the platform** — Use the same prompts across datasets, simulations, and experiments for consistent behaviour and evaluation.
