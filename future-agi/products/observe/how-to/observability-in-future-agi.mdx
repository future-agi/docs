Observability in the Future AGI project is designed to provide comprehensive insights into the behavior and performance of AI models. It is structured around two primary components: 

<CardGroup>
  <Card 
    title="Experiment" 
    icon="flask"

  >
    Run experiments on your LLM application to find the best fit for your use case before deploying 
  </Card>

  <Card 
    title="Observe" 
    icon="chart-mixed" 
    href="/future-agi/products/observe/how-to/observe"
  >
    Monitor and analyze the performance of your LLM application post-deployment
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
