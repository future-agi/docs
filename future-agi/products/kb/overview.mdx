The Knowledge Base is a centralized document management system that transforms static information into actionable intelligence. It stores, organizes, and makes accessible a wide variety of organizational documents through advanced processing and retrieval capabilities.

Our system supports key document types including:

- FAQs and troubleshooting guides
- Technical documentation and manuals  
- SOPs and process workflows
- Training materials and HR policies
- Legal documents and compliance information
- Product descriptions and specifications
- Historical records and research data

The Knowledge Base enables powerful use cases:

- **Synthetic Data Generation**: Create high-quality training examples from your documents to fine-tune LLMs
- **Contextual Retrieval**: Provide relevant information to AI systems for more accurate and grounded responses
- **Hallucination Detection**: Identify when AI generates information not supported by source materials
