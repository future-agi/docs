The Knowledge Base feature provides a structured way to manage and utilise documents for evaluations, analysis, and synthetic data generation. It allows users to upload documents in various formats, process them for indexing, and retrieve relevant information through queries.

With this feature, users can:

- **Upload documents** in formats like PDF, DOCX, TXT, and RTF.
- **Process and index** the content for efficient retrieval.
- **Generate data** that can be used to fine tune LLMs.
- **Evaluate information** through content analysis, comparison, and verification methods.

The Knowledge Base is designed for researchers, analysts, and organizations that need to efficiently access, manage, and evaluate large volumes of information.
