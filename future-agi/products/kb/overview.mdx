The Knowledge Base feature provides a structured way to manage and utilise documents like FAQs, troubleshooting guides, how-to guides, tutorials, technical documentation, manuals, standard operating procedures (SOPs), process documentation, product descriptions, training materials, HR policies, legal documents, and historical records for evaluations and synthetic data generation. It allows users to upload documents in various formats, process them for indexing, and retrieve relevant contexts through queries.

With this feature, users can:

- **Upload documents** in formats like PDF, DOCX, TXT, and RTF.
- **Process and index** the content for efficient retrieval.
- **Generate data** that can be used to fine tune LLMs.
- **Evaluate Outputs of LLMs** in case of RAG based applications.

The Knowledge Base is designed for researchers, analysts, and organizations that need to efficiently access, manage information and evaluate LLMs using them.
