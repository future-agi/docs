## How to choose the winner?

1. Go to the [Prototype](https://app.futureagi.com/prototype) page.
2. Click on the `Choose Winner` button.
3. Adjust the sliders for each metric to indicate its importance on a scale from 0 (not important) to 10 (very important).
4. Based on the values you set for each metric, all prototype versions are ranked.
5. The version with the highest overall score, considering all metrics, is selected as the winner.
