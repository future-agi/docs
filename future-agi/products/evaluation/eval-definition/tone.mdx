title: "Tone"
---

**Definition**

Tone evaluation analyses the tone and sentiment of content. This evaluation helps in understanding the emotional context and intent behind the text, which is crucial for tailoring communication to specific audiences or purposes.

---
**Calculation**

The evaluation process involves classifying the tone of the input text from a predefined list of choices, such as neutral, joy, love, fear, surprise, sadness, anger, annoyance, and confusion. The system uses sentiment analysis models to determine the tone and provides a list of identified tones.

---
**What to do If you get Undesired Tone in Content**

Adjust the tone of the content to align with the intended emotional context or communication goal, ensuring it is appropriate for the audience and purpose. 

Utilise tone analysis to refine messaging, making it more engaging, professional, or empathetic as needed. Continuously improve tone detection models to enhance their ability to recognize and interpret nuanced emotional expressions, leading to more accurate and context-aware assessments.
