- **TURING_LARGE** `turing_large`: Best suited for complex evaluations that require deep understanding and nuanced analysis of the data.

- **TURING_SMALL** `turing_small`: Optimized for simple evaluations that require basic understanding of the data with faster processing.

- **TURING_FLASH** `turing_flash`: Ultra-fast model for simple evaluations that require basic understanding with minimal latency.

- **PROTECT** `protect`: Designed for evaluations focused on safety and content moderation tasks with superfast response times.
 
- **PROTECT_FLASH** `protect_flash`: Lightweight model for safety evaluations with rapid response times.
