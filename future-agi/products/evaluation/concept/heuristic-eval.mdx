---

---

### Why Use Heuristic Eval?

- **No Need of Ground-Truth Labels:** Heuristic evals are particularly useful when reference annotations are unavailable or infeasible to produce.
- **Cost-Effective:** Unlike human-in-the-loop or model-based eval approaches, heuristic metrics are computationally lightweight and can be executed at scale.
- **Deterministic Output:** Heuristic evals provide clear deterministic outputs that can be easily interpreted by developers and integrated into automated quality assurance systems.
