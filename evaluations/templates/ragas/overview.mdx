---
title: 'RAGAS Overview'
description: 'The RAGAS Evaluation suite is a comprehensive evaluation tool designed to ensure the reliability, accuracy, and quality of Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems.'
---

RAGAS is a framework for evaluating the quality of LLM models in the context of Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG). It provides a set of metrics to evaluate the accuracy, relevance, and other aspects of the generated responses.
